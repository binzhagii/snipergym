About Us

Sniper Gym was founded by MMA Champion Stephen Loman as a haven for those who are looking to get fit.
We know that building a positive culture is incredibly important.
We believe in encouraging, supporting, challenging, learning and growing to be the best!
Our flexible working solutions, gym rebates and educational opportunities create a positive work/life balance for all our employees.
